Recipe for Caprese Stuffed Avocados

Description

Caprese stuffed avocados are a fresh and delightful twist on the traditional Caprese salad, combining the creamy richness of ripe avocados with the classic flavors of Italy. To prepare this dish, avocados are halved and pitted, then filled with a mixture of juicy cherry tomatoes, fresh mozzarella balls, and fragrant basil leaves. The filling is typically drizzled with a balsamic glaze or a splash of olive oil, adding a tangy sweetness that complements the creamy avocado and the savory, milky mozzarella. This dish is not only visually appealing, with its vibrant green, red, and white color palette, but also offers a satisfying balance of textures and flavors. Caprese stuffed avocados make for a perfect light meal or an impressive appetizer, celebrating the simplicity and freshness of Mediterranean cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and cut in half
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella pearls
  • 1/4 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 6 fresh basil leaves, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Step 1: Arrange avocado halves onto a plate.
  2. Step 2: Toss together mozzarella pearls, cherry tomatoes, and basil in a small bowl. Spoon the Caprese filling into each avocado half and drizzle with balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately.
